üîß Maintenance Schedule

üå°Ô∏è Furnace Filter

Every 3 months in Quebec

Replace your furnace filter regularly to maintain air quality and system efficiency. In Quebec's climate with heating season running 6+ months, change every 3 months (or monthly during peak winter if you have pets/allergies).

Check your current filter size before ordering! Common sizes: 16x25x4, 20x25x4

üõí Buy Furnace Filters (20x25x4)

üíß GeneralAire Humidifier Pad

Annually (Start of heating season)

Replace the humidifier water panel/pad at the beginning of each heating season (September/October). A clogged or mineral-encrusted pad reduces efficiency and can harbor bacteria.

Also check: Clean the water distribution tray and ensure the drain line is clear.

üõí Buy GeneralAire Replacement Pad

üåÄ Aldes Air Exchanger Filter

Every 3-6 months

Clean or replace the air exchanger filters every 3-6 months depending on use and air quality. Dirty filters reduce efficiency and air exchange effectiveness.

How to clean: Most filters can be vacuumed or washed with mild soap and water. Let dry completely before reinstalling.

üö∞ Check Drains

Every 3 months

Humidifier drain: Ensure water flows freely. Clear any mineral buildup or algae growth.

Furnace condensate drain: Check for clogs, especially if you have a high-efficiency furnace. A clogged drain can cause water damage or system shutdown.

Air exchanger drain: In winter, the HRV produces condensation. Ensure drain line is clear and not frozen.

üí° Pro Tip: Set calendar reminders for these maintenance tasks. Regular maintenance prevents costly repairs and ensures optimal performance year-round.

How They Work Together

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL RULE:
Aldes controller humidity setting should ALWAYS be 10-15% HIGHER than GeneralAire setting

Why They Have Different Jobs:

GeneralAire Humidifier:
- ADDS moisture
- Sets your desired comfort level
- Example: 30% in winter (minimum for wood protection)
Aldes Controller HRV Humidity Trigger:
- REMOVES moisture (via ventilation)
- Safety valve for excess humidity
- Example: 40% in winter

What Happens If You Match Them:

If both are set to 30%:
- Humidifier tries to maintain 30%
- Aldes controller starts removing moisture at 30%
- They fight each other
- Result: Dry air + wasted energy

Correct Settings:

üè† Wood Protection Note: Maintaining 30-50% humidity year-round prevents wood shrinkage, cracking, and structural stress. Never let humidity drop below 30% in winter!
